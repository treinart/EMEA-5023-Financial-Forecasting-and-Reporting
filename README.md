## Module 2: Understanding Financial Statements

**Goal:** Analyze the income statement, balance sheet, and statement of cash flows for a public company, then explain what the statements say about the company's financial position.

**Company selected:** Trane Technologies plc (NYSE: TT)

**Reason for selection:** Thermo King is part of Trane Technologies and is a primary vendor in my work. The financial health of Trane Technologies matters because it can affect vendor reliability, equipment availability, warranty support, parts supply, and long-term continuity.

**Primary deliverables**
- Jupyter Notebook report.
- HTML export for GitHub Pages.
- Supporting screenshots from the annual report.
- Source workbook with organized financial values.
- Public Trane Technologies filings and investor presentation materials used for citation and review.

---

## Analysis Highlights

- **2025 net revenues:** $21,321.9 million.
- **2025 operating income:** $3,967.4 million.
- **2025 net earnings attributable to Trane Technologies:** $2,918.6 million.
- **2025 operating cash flow:** $3,194.5 million.
- **Current ratio:** 1.25.
- **Quick ratio:** 0.92.
- **Gross margin:** 36.2%.
- **Operating margin:** 18.6%.
- **Net margin:** 13.7%.

The report concludes that Trane Technologies appears financially healthy based on the 2025 statements reviewed. The main watch items are leverage, operating cash flow growth, and whether service revenue continues to expand.
